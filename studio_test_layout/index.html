What is Blitzpanel Studio?

Blitzpanel Studio is an online tool for designing and sourcing industrial control panels. Think of it as CAD + sourcing + instant quoting in one place. No more juggling spreadsheets, catalogs, and emails.

Is this just software?

Blitzpanel Studio is an online tool for designing and sourcing industrial control panels. Think of it as CAD + sourcing + instant quoting in one place. No more juggling spreadsheets, catalogs, and emails.

Do I have to send my design to your factory?

No. You can use Blitzpanel purely as a design and sourcing tool i.e. create block diagrams, schematics, or panel layouts and generate your BOM. If you’re ready for production and don’t want to use our factory, you can export your design and send it to your preferred shop.

Can I use my own components?

Yes. Blitzpanel has its growing component library that’s closely coupled with our ERP, but you can also import your own parts or request that we add missing ones. Note: custom components will affect the end quote and estimated ship date.

How accurate are the quotes?

Quotes are instant and based on current component pricing, wiring, and panel build heuristics. For unusual parts or custom enclosures, we’ll flag items that need manual confirmation.

Do I need CAD experience?

Not necessarily. Blitzpanel is designed for electrical engineers and technicians, but the drag-and-drop layout and 3D viewer make it approachable even if you’ve never touched an ECAD tool.

Is Blitzpanel only for UL-508A shops?

No. The tool is useful for anyone designing control panels from OEM engineers to startups, but we’re building toward UL-compliant outputs so that shops can go straight to production without re-drawing.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your designs and BOMs remain private to your account unless you explicitly share them. We don’t sell design data.
